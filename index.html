Mission and Vision

Mission and Vision

Vision
To create a conducive atmosphere for the growth of quality of sports among the young sports persons by imparting knowledge, skill and other qualities through systematic and scientiic sports tranining

Mission
To Indentify, pick up and train the young potential sports persons and their sympathizers/supporters and enable them to climb up the ladder of unlimited excellence relentlessly.

Basic Sports Academy

Basic Sports Academy

It has been accepted by the people of India that Manipur is the power-house of sports. But India could not produce significant number of sports persons of international standard in spite of having large population. Manipur could produce a few sports persons of international standards though Manipur has got young sports talents, the state could not promote them to the International Standards due to the lack of various sports infrastructures such a sports academy/institutions, playgrounds, skilled coaches and advanced equipments, etc. These infrastructures should be developed from the grassroot levels viz. village-levels, block-levels and district-levels. Though the government has been attempting to develop such infrastructures, these are still inadequate. To supplement the efforts of the government in this line the active participation of the private sectors viz. individuals, organisations, corporate bodies, NGO's, etc. is the need of the hour.

Imphal west district has no sports academy/school/institution at the private sector. Therefore, it is felt to establish a "BASIC SPORTS ACADEMY" (BSA) in Imphal West district as a sole proprietorship concern.

Basic Unique School

Basic Unique School

The academy will sponsor/adopt a separate school for general education having an independent entity under the name/title of "BASIC UNIQUE SCHOOL" (BUS). This separate school will function within the guidelines of the appropriate state or central government authority. However, the school may exercise certain discretions in its functioning to suit the local requirements for the welfare of the students of general education of the students and parents-teachers-administrators' relationship.

The following main activities will be taken up by the school authority:

  1. There will be a separate Governing Body of the school.
  2. There will be a steering committee of the school consisting of selected school teachers, administrators and parents/guardians of the students.
  3. The Prospectus, annual school calendars, fee structures, academic curriculum/sessions, etc. will be as per guidelines of the appropriate authorities with which the school is affiliated.

Objectives

Objectives

  1. To identify young sports talents at the district level and impart them basic sports knowledge and skills.
  2. To arrange for further advancement in their future sports career to attain international standards by tying up with the more advanced national and international institutions.
  3. To go hand-in-hand with national sports policies framed by the from time to time.
